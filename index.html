My journey

Built from scratch,

```

Iași, Romania

Where it all began

I grew up in Romania, studied Computer Science, and became curious, ambitious and already a little restless. I fell in love with problem-solving, and with the man who would become my husband.

Munich, Germany

The first leap

At 23, I packed up and moved to Munich with my partner. Two Romanians figuring out a foreign city, a new language, and how to be a couple outside everything familiar. We stuck together, found work, made friends, and grew.

2017

Barcelona, Spain

My favourite city, my real home

I had always loved Barcelona. After four years in Munich, I followed that feeling and moved to Spain. Starting over completely, learning Catalan and Spanish, and building a life I truly chose.

2019

Barcelona, Spain

Discovering Product Management

I stepped into a PM role and instantly felt it click. Challenging people, finding solutions together, leading without authority. It was the perfect shape for how my mind works.

2020

Barcelona, Spain

A daughter, a new home, a pandemic

I welcomed my daughter, bought my first home and navigated renovations, all during the pandemic and without any outside help. Just me, my partner, and the life we kept on building.

2022

Barcelona, Spain

Leading people

A new chapter: product leadership. Managing a team, mentoring others, and learning how to hold space for people while still delivering. A whole new kind of challenge.

2023

Barcelona, Spain

Co-founding a community

I co-founded Ladies in Product and discovered an unexpected love for community building and bringing people together.

"I have always believed that when women support each other, extraordinary things happen. Ladies in Product was born from that belief."

2025

Barcelona, Spain

A son, and a conscious reset

My son arrived in April. I chose to return to individual contributor work, not as a step back, but as a deliberate act of protecting my energy, my joy, and the life I have built.
```

The principles

Courage to begin again

02

Collaboration over everything

I do not solve problems for people. I solve them with people, always looking for the answer that belongs to the whole room, not just to me.

Happy woman, happy home

I take care of my family, my community, the planet and myself. I know these are not in competition. They are the same circle.
